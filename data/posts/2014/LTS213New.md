eXist Solutions GmbH is proud to announce the release of eXist LTS 2.1.3. The release only contains hand-selected, tested bug fixes and improvements and is full backwards compatibil to the eXist LTS 2.1 production-ready branch. 


The release mainly focuses on performance enhancements like the faster insertion or addition of nodes using update extensions and impovements of the eXist Optimizer. Other changes like improved error messages and standard error codes also focus on the needs of enterprise-scale users that require a reliable and supported version of the eXistdb open source version. 


eXist LTS is available as a subscription at [http://exist-db.org](http://exist-db.org). Subscribers may download version 2.1.3 from the Customer Portal.
