Because most people in the eXist core team are either traveling or are overloaded with work, we unfortunately have to cancel this year's eXist preconference event at XML Prague. The good news is that we're busy working on more eXist-related projects than ever, which made our joint available time too constrained to send the whole team to Prague. We have thus decided to invest the time we would have spent there into finally getting a release out. We also promise to post some of the neat stuff we planned to show in Prague on this blog instead. So please look forward to more soon.
