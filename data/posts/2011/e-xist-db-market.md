eXist-db Market is a comming crowdsourced community interaction model to bring easiness into the connection of user ideas to sponsors and developers.

This model lets the users to suggest and vote on features that they like work on. These prioritized features can then be taken on on by one or more sponsors. Willing developers can then go on with specifying and implement the sponsored task at hand.

Please don't hesitate to provide to the baselining of this by sending your suggestions to info -at- exist-db.org with []() in subject.
