In current trunk (starting 2010-10-01) and in the upcoming 1.4.x release the WebDAV interface has been reimplemented using the [Milton WebDAV](http://milton.ettrema.com%20) framework.

The new implementation is considered better (more stable and better clients compatibility) than the original implementation.

The documentation will be updated soon.
